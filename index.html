Process

Free Estimate

The first step in our process is to schedule a free estimate. During this appointment, one of our experts will visit your property to assess your needs and discuss your vision for your roofing.

Quote

Based on the information gathered during the free estimate, we will provide you with a detailed quote for the services you require. This quote will outline the scope of work, materials, and estimated timeline for your project.

Sign & Agreement

Once you have reviewed and accepted the quote, you will be asked to sign a contract to formalize the agreement between you and our company.

Construction

Our team will begin the construction process, working closely with you to ensure that the project is completed according to your specifications and timeline.

Pre-Delivery Inspection

Before completing the project, we will conduct a pre-delivery inspection to ensure that the work meets our high standards and your expectations. If any final adjustments are needed, we will make them at this time.
